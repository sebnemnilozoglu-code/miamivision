Why "The Magic City"?

Founded in 1896 by Julia Tuttle, Miami is the only major U.S. city conceived by a woman. It earned its nickname because it transformed from a tiny settlement of 300 people to a booming metropolis in just one year. Today, it stands as a $350B+ economy.
